Converting them into customers through UX

I can support you during a redesign in creating ergonomic and intuitive interfaces that will encourage your visitors to become loyal customers. Through regular monitoring, I focus on your users' behaviour towards the site, identifying friction points and helping you improve your customers' journey. To monitor your website's performance, we choose key performance indicators (KPIs) adapted to your objectives together. Among them: your real visibility on platforms, traffic received, bounce and engagement rates, time spent on the site, and conversions achieved. This data will enable us to measure the impact of actions implemented and identify new areas for improvement. The iterative method guarantees you continuous improvement.
